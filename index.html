Why It Matters

Unmanaged procurement is where organizations get hurt.

Late bidders gaining unfair access. Scope documents leaking to favored contractors. Boards approving bids they don't fully understand. These aren't hypotheticals — they're how capital projects go wrong every day.

We exist to close those gaps. Inductra brings Fortune 500 procurement rigor to organizations that deserve it most, translating complex bid processes into clear, trustworthy decisions your leadership can stand behind.

"Inductra operates as your advocate — not the contractor's."
⚠️

Unfair Bid Access

Without structure, late or favored bidders gain access to scope information others don't have. Inductra controls the process so every qualified contractor competes on equal footing.

⚠️

Scope Leakage

Informal relationships between staff and contractors can compromise bid integrity before a single number is submitted. We insulate your organization completely.

⚠️

Uninformed Decisions

Boards, vestries, and finance committees shouldn't have to decode contractor proposals. We present bids in plain language so your leadership can make a confident, informed decision.

The Process

From idea to contract — fair and fast

A disciplined four-step process that brings technical rigor and procurement integrity to every capital project. No shortcuts. No favorites. No hassle.

📋

Intake & Scope

We meet with your team, assess the project, and develop a complete technical scope of work — on your behalf, at no charge.

Weeks 1–2
📢

RFP Publication

Your project is published to our contractor network with specifications, timelines, and evaluation criteria fully defined.

Weeks 3–4
⚖️

Bid Review & Recommendation

We evaluate all submissions on technical merit, qualifications, and price — then present a clear recommendation with full documentation.

Weeks 5–6
✅

Owner Selection

You review our recommendation and make the final contractor selection. You sign the contract. Our work is complete.

Week 7
🤝

Fair & Open

Every qualified contractor gets a fair shot. No predetermined winners, no informal arrangements. The best bid wins on merit.

🔒

Strictly Confidential

All submitted bids are kept strictly confidential throughout the process. No contractor sees another's pricing — ever.

📊

Technically Rigorous

Evaluations are based on documented criteria — scope compliance, qualifications, price, and schedule. No guesswork.

💡

No Upfront or Advisory Fee to Your Organization

Inductra charges no upfront or advisory fee to your organization. Our administrative fee is included in the awarded contract value and paid directly by the winning contractor. Your organization receives professional procurement management at no cost — and contractors benefit from access to qualified, pre-approved institutional projects.
