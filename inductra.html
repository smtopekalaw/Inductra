Zero-Cost Model for Organizations

Inductra charges no fees to churches, schools, or faith-based organizations. Our advisory fee is a percentage of the awarded contract value, paid directly by the winning contractor. Your organization receives professional procurement management at no cost — and contractors benefit from access to qualified, pre-approved institutional projects.
